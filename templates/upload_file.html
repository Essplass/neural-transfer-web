How to use!

1 • Select an artstyle from the dropdown box at the top

2 • Next, select a JPG or PNG image from your local machine, note unsupported files will not work and you will be prompted with an error at the top center of the page

3 • Press the green upload button to upload your work into the deep learning model

4 • You will be redirected to a webpage with your edited image, the style transfer is complete

5 • To return to the upload page simply press the green button
